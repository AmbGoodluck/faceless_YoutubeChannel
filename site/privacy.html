Privacy Policy — Lights Out Tales

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This application ("the App", operated under the channel Lights Out Tales) is a personal content-publishing tool used solely by its owner to publish the owner's own original short videos to the owner's own social media accounts. It is not offered to, or used by, any third-party users.

What information the App accesses

When you authorize the App with TikTok, it accesses:

  • Basic profile information (user.info.basic) — your TikTok display name and open ID, used only to confirm which account content is being uploaded to.
  • Video upload permission (video.upload) — used to upload video files that the owner created, into the owner's own TikTok account as drafts for the owner to review and publish.

How information is used

The App uses these permissions only to upload the owner's own original videos to the owner's own account. The App does not read, collect, store, sell, or share any personal data with any third party. Access tokens are stored locally on the owner's own computer and are used only to communicate with TikTok's official API.

Data retention

No user data is retained on any server. Authorization tokens live only on the owner's local machine and can be revoked at any time from the owner's TikTok account settings.

Third parties

The App shares no data with third parties. It communicates only with TikTok's official APIs and (for the owner's own publishing) YouTube's official APIs.
